Coding Lab Talk: Ana Meisel

Monday April 22nd 2024
@ 3:00pm - 4:00pm

Red Square, 370 Jay St. 4th Floor

Ana Meisel is a web developer and net art curator from Czechia, based in London, UK. She builds websites as part of 4 Us & 4 Others studio, curates and develops internet art gallery External Pages, and teaches design at Goldsmiths, University of London. She is one-half of the alternative tech history podcast Our Friend The Computer and one-third of the research group Superkilogirls.
